About


I'm a 3rd year student currently pursuing Computer Security at York University. I love to join hackathons because hackathons allow me to learn new tech concepts within a short period and build projects that solve real-world problems. The first time I got exposed to hackathon was my freshman year at university. Even since then, I am passionate to encourage people to join hackathons.

I'm also passionate about communities, a place to make new connections and learn new skills. Through participating in Local Hack Day and hackathons hosted by Major League Hacking, I have a chance to meet new people and learn new tech stack.

I was born and raised in Vietnam but currently study in Canada as an international student. Other than English, I can speak Cantonese and Vietnamese. During free time, I often translate Webtoon comics from English to Vietnamese.
